About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Grace Kane I am a B.S. Aeronautics student at Saint Louis University with business aviation internship experience. I am a commercial pilot for single-engine land airplanes and I possess an instrument rating. I am a passionate and a dedicated student motivated to positively contribute to business aviation.

Beyond my passion for aviation, I enjoy volleyball, golf, community service, literature, podcasts, fitness and travel. Most of all, I enjoy sharing my love for aviation with others and I am enthusiatic to inspire and mentor the next generation of aviators.

    As a Pilot Intern at Parker, I worked closely with company pilots during the flight planning process and I observed in-flight operations to become knowledgeable about corporate flight department operations.
